1. Shrimp and Broccoli Stir-Fry
Ingredients Needed:

Your Ingredients: Shrimp, frozen broccoli, brown rice (or quinoa), hummus (optional for dipping).
Additional Ingredients: Soy sauce, garlic, ginger, olive oil or sesame oil, red pepper flakes (optional).
Instructions:

Sauté minced garlic and ginger in oil.
Add shrimp and cook until pink.
Toss in broccoli and cook until tender.
Serve over brown rice or quinoa, drizzled with soy sauce.
2. Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas and Guacamole
Ingredients Needed:

Your Ingredients: Quinoa, chickpeas, wholly guacamole, frozen green peas, baby cut carrots, goat cheese.
Additional Ingredients: Lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper, diced tomatoes or bell peppers (optional).
Instructions:

Cook quinoa as per package instructions.
In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, chickpeas, green peas, chopped carrots, and goat cheese.
Dress with lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Add guacamole on top before serving.
3. Breakfast Protein Bowl
Ingredients Needed:

Your Ingredients: Kodiak protein oats, eggs, Greek yogurt, frozen berries, banana slices.
Additional Ingredients: Honey or maple syrup (optional), cinnamon (optional).
Instructions:

Cook oats as per package instructions, adding water or milk.

Serve oats topped with Greek yogurt, frozen berries, banana slices, and drizzle with honey or syrup if desired.
4. Turkey and Vegetable Stir-Fry
Ingredients Needed:

Your Ingredients: Ground turkey, frozen butternut squash, frozen green peas, baby cut carrots, black beans (optional).
Additional Ingredients: Soy sauce, garlic, onion, bell pepper, olive oil.
Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan and sauté diced onion and garlic.
Add ground turkey and cook until browned.
Toss in butternut squash, peas, and carrots; stir-fry until cooked through.
Optionally, add black beans for extra protein.
5. Tuna and Sweet Potato Cakes
Ingredients Needed:

Your Ingredients: Canned tuna, sweet potato, eggs, breadcrumbs (if needed).
Additional Ingredients: Onion, garlic powder, salt, pepper, lemon juice.
Instructions:

Boil and mash the sweet potato.
In a bowl, combine tuna, mashed sweet potato, eggs, and seasonings.
Form into patties and pan-fry until golden brown on both sides.
6. Creamy Shrimp Pasta
Ingredients Needed:

Your Ingredients: Shrimp, whole-wheat pasta, goat cheese (for creaminess), frozen broccoli.
Additional Ingredients: Olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, parsley or basil (optional).
Instructions:

Cook whole-wheat pasta according to package directions.
In a separate pan, sauté garlic in olive oil, add shrimp and cook until done.
Add cooked pasta, broccoli, and goat cheese; stir until creamy and warm.
Finish with a squeeze of lemon juice and herbs.
